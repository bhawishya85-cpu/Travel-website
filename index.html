ABOUT ALASKA

Alaska is defined by its vast wilderness and towering mountain ranges, most notably the Alaska Range, home to Denali—the highest peak in North America at 20,310 feet. Stretching nearly 600 miles across the state, the Alaska Range is a dramatic landscape of rugged peaks, sweeping glaciers, and pristine valleys.

To make the most of your vacation experience, you'll want to plan your itinerary and activities to take advantage of the best weather and optimal conditions. Remember, the Arctic terrain is located at the heart of Alaska's vast wilderness.
